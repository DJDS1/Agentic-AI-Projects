🎯 STRATEGY OBJECTIVE

Generate >20% annual ROI on a $100,000 futures portfolio trading ES, NQ, YM, GC, SI, CL, NG, ZB, ZN, ZF. Risk 1–2% per trade ($1,000–$2,000). Minimum 2:1 R:R per trade. Trade only HIGH and MEDIUM conviction setups confirmed on at least 2 of 3 timeframes (Monthly/Weekly/Daily). The stress test at 32.5% win rate still produced +78.33% ROI due to a 2.73:1 actual R:R, demonstrating the strategy's positive expected value.

📋 Daily Pre-Market Routine

  • 6:00 AM Review overnight futures gaps vs prior close
  • 6:15 AM Check economic calendar (NFP, CPI, EIA, Fed speakers)
  • 6:30 AM Run Pre-Market PDF Report — update bias scorecard
  • 7:00 AM Identify top 2-3 setups from HIGH conviction list
  • 7:30 AM Set entry orders, stops, and profit targets in platform
  • 8:30 AM Re-evaluate if major macro data released pre-open
  • 9:25 AM Final check — no trades in first 5 minutes (opening range)
  • POST Log all trades same day; update portfolio tracker

⚙️ Trade Entry Rules

  • RULE 1 Only enter after confirmation on Daily + Weekly timeframe
  • RULE 2 Wait for pullback to support — never chase ATH breakouts
  • RULE 3 MACD must be trending in trade direction on Daily
  • RULE 4 RSI must not be overbought (>75) for longs or oversold (<25) for shorts
  • RULE 5 Volume must be above 20-period average on entry candle
  • RULE 6 No trades within 30 min of major economic releases
  • RULE 7 Max 3 concurrent open positions across all contracts
  • RULE 8 Correlated contracts count as 1 (ES + NQ = 1 position)

🛑 Stop Loss & Exit Rules

  • EXIT 1 Hard stop always set at order entry — no mental stops
  • EXIT 2 Move stop to break-even once trade reaches 1:1 R:R
  • EXIT 3 Take 50% off at 2:1 target; trail remaining with ATR stop
  • EXIT 4 Full exit if price closes against trade direction on Daily
  • EXIT 5 Time-based exit: close any trade open more than 5 days
  • EXIT 6 Daily loss limit: if down 3% in a day, stop trading that day
  • EXIT 7 Weekly loss limit: if down 5% in a week, review before trading

📐 Position Sizing Rules

  • SIZE 1 HIGH conviction: 1.5–2% risk ($1,500–$2,000)
  • SIZE 2 MEDIUM conviction: 1% risk ($1,000)
  • SIZE 3 LOW conviction: 0.5% risk — avoid entirely if possible
  • SIZE 4 After 3 consecutive losses: halve position size for next 5 trades
  • SIZE 5 After new portfolio high: can scale to 2% on HIGH conviction
  • SIZE 6 Max total risk across all open positions: 5% of portfolio

⚠️ Strategy Review Triggers

  • REVIEW 1 Win rate drops below 25% over 30-trade rolling window
  • REVIEW 2 Drawdown exceeds 10% from portfolio peak
  • REVIEW 3 Monthly ROI below 0% for 2 consecutive months
  • REVIEW 4 Any single contract with grade D for 2 consecutive months → pause it
  • REVIEW 5 Profit factor drops below 1.2 over 50-trade window
  • REVIEW 6 Cumulative ROI tracking 10%+ behind the monthly target pace
